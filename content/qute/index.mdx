Qute is a templating engine designed specifically to meet the [Quarkus](https://quarkus.io/) needs. The usage of reflection is minimized to reduce the size of native images. The API combines both the imperative and the non-blocking reactive style of coding. In the development mode, all files located in ***src/main/resources/templates*** are watched for changes and modifications are immediately visible.
